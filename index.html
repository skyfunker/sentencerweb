Features

Built-in Lessons

List of 10 built-in lessons. Switching between the face and back side of the card (question and answer).

Cards navigation

Back and forth navigation between cards within a lesson. Navigation to specific card by its position.

Cards shuffling

You can shuffle cards in random order.

Cards marking

You can mark any card and then play only with marked cards.

Text-to-speech support

You can listen to the card text (Internet connection required)

Lesson state saving

App saves and restores your previous lesson session (last position, marked cards) automatically.

Custom Lessons

You can prepare your own set of cards, upload it using special form and download the lesson scanning generated QR-code.

Create your lesson

If you have Sentencer Android application installed on your device, you can utilize this form to create your own Sentencer lesson.
Prepare text file according to these requirements, upload the file and scan generated QR-code to download the file from the server.
